\section{Integrating Technology into Product Strategy}

Technology should not just enable product strategy—it should drive it. As a \gls{CTO}, your goal is to make technology a core part of the product's value proposition.

\subsection{Technology as a Differentiator}

In a competitive market, use cutting-edge technology as a differentiator. This could mean leveraging machine learning for better personalization or using blockchain to improve transparency and trust.

\subsection{Scalability and Reliability}

Products that fail under load are a business failure, not just a technical one. Ensure your architecture is designed for growth and resilience from the beginning.

\subsection{Innovation and Experimentation}

Foster a culture where engineering teams can experiment. Dedicate time for research and development to keep your product ahead of the competition.

\subsection{Data-Driven Product Development}

Use data to inform product decisions. Your infrastructure must be able to collect and analyze user behavior to guide your development efforts.
