\section{Data-Driven Decision Making}

Metrics are essential for tracking the success of your product and technical decisions. Choose the metrics that matter and use them to guide your strategy.

\subsection{Metrics That Matter}

A successful product and technical strategy requires measuring the right things.

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{North Star Metric:} A single metric that represents the company's long-term success.
    \item \textbf{Customer Retention Rate:} Indicates the long-term value of your product.
    \item \textbf{Technical Performance:} System uptime, response times, and error rates.
    \item \textbf{Development Velocity:} Sprint completion rates and cycle times.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Scaling Successfully}

Scalability is not just a technical challenge—it's a business enabler. Ensure your infrastructure can scale without outpacing your growth.

\begin{itemize}
    \item Design for horizontal and vertical scalability.
    \item Use cloud infrastructure for elasticity.
    \item Optimize database performance for high-load scenarios.
    \item Implement effective caching strategies.
\end{itemize}
